1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register or create an account on the Xili app.
  • Express interest in our Services (e.g., via contact forms or inquiries).
  • Participate in app activities (e.g., posting content, entering competitions).
  • Contact us directly.

This may include:

  • Email addresses
  • Usernames
  • Names
  • Social media login data (e.g., name, email, profile picture) if you register via social media accounts (see Section 5).
  • Other information you provide, such as feedback or contest entries.

All information you provide must be accurate, complete, and updated as needed.

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain technical data when you use our Services, such as:

  • Device Data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, and app usage data (e.g., features used, time spent).
  • Website Data: For our static website (www.xiliapp.com), we may collect IP addresses, browser details, and page visit data via analytics tools for usage trends and site performance.
  • Push Notification Data: If you opt-in to push notifications in the Xili app, we collect data to deliver account-related or feature-specific alerts. You can opt out via your device settings.

We do not collect sensitive personal information, such as racial or ethnic origins, sexual orientation, or religious beliefs.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your personal information to:

  • Facilitate account creation and management: Enable registration, login, and account functionality in the Xili app.
  • Deliver Services: Provide app features, such as content sharing or notifications.
  • Communicate with you: Respond to inquiries or send updates about our Services.
  • Administer promotions: Manage contests, prize draws, or rewards programs.
  • Improve our Services: Analyze usage trends to enhance app functionality, user experience, and marketing efforts.
  • Ensure security: Prevent fraud, detect unauthorized access, and protect our Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations: Meet requirements under applicable laws (e.g., tax or data protection regulations).

We process your information only when we have a valid legal basis, such as your consent, a contract, or legal obligations.

3. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Personal Information?

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Business Transfers: During mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, your information may be transferred to another company, subject to applicable laws.
  • Offer Wall Providers: The Xili app may include a third-party “offer wall” offering rewards (e.g., virtual currency) for completing advertisement offers. Your user ID or similar identifier may be shared with these providers to prevent fraud and credit rewards.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors (e.g., analytics or hosting providers) to support our Services, but only under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share information to comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights.

Our Services may link to third-party websites or services (e.g., via advertisements or offer walls). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties. Review their privacy policies before sharing information. Links to third-party sites do not imply our endorsement.

5. How Do We Handle Your Social Logins?

If you register or log in to the Xili app using a social media account (e.g., Facebook, X), we may receive profile information from the provider, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Public profile details (e.g., friends list, if applicable)

We use this information only for account creation, login, and purposes outlined in this policy. We do not control how social media providers use your data, so review their privacy policies for details.

6. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes in this policy, such as:

  • Maintaining your account (until you delete it).
  • Fulfilling legal, tax, or accounting requirements.

When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize your data. If deletion is not possible (e.g., in backup archives), we securely store it until deletion is feasible.

7. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and secure servers. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee protection against unauthorized access (e.g., hacking). Use our Services in a secure environment and keep your credentials confidential.

8. Do We Collect Information from Minors?

We do not knowingly collect or solicit data from users under 18 years old. If we discover we have collected data from a minor, we will deactivate their account and delete their information promptly. Contact us at contact@xiliapp.com if you believe we have collected data from a minor.

9. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Depending on your location (e.g., EU under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have rights to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correct: Update inaccurate data.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object: Limit or oppose certain processing activities.
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a transferable format.
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke consent for processing (without affecting prior lawful processing).

To exercise these rights, visit www.xiliapp.com or email contact@xiliapp.com. We will respond within the timeframes required by law (e.g., 30 days under GDPR, 45 days under CCPA).

Account Management

  • Update or review: Log in to your Xili app account to update your information.
  • Delete: Request account deletion via the app or by contacting us. We will delete or anonymize your data, except where required for legal purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
